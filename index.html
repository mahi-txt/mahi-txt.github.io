airplane 1
Posted on 2024-05-26 at 4:07 AM

Last night, I spent an hour and a half transporting myself to a more significant city. Sunday night. Why am I here? About 7 living people I saw while in said city for 45 minutes. 5 of which were also American university students. Why are we here? 2 of which were locking doors to their respective art gallery and convenience store. Quietest place I've experienced in a very long time. Standing between a cathedral and a pharmacy. Silent, I am the loudest thing around. I am a headless chicken. I spent an hour returning to the airport hotel room in which a monkey hanging from a rope awaited me. A light fixture. The loneliest I've felt in a while was 11 pm on a summer Sunday in the center of Munich. I said to someone later that night that I would film a movie here.

beauty, essex, & an apology for party rocking!!
Posted on 2024-05-31 at 8:23 AM

Some moments feel transitory and sometimes I ascribe their beauty to the fact that they must end. Suffering, though, isn’t defined by my belief in its end. I give it power to consume all, to give me a silent permission to abdicate responsibility, rhyme, and reason. I give it more of my space and time in hopes that if we let it grow, it will go away. I let it grow but I am no longer shocked, moved, or surprised by it. It happens and it’s a piece of me.
I suffer, I give it time, I heal for the rest of my life. I love, I run away from it, I regret it for the rest of my life. I look back and think it was beautiful and will I find it again?
Suffering is the tree that keeps growing right under me and beauty is the fleeting bird I am raised closer to. I carry the hope that we belong to the same branch.
